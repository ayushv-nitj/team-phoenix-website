About Team Phoenix

Team Phoenix is the official Aero-Designing and Aeromodelling team of NIT Jamshedpur, comprised of a passionate group of UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) enthusiasts. They specialize in designing, fabricating, and innovating advanced UAVs and drones for various applications. The team has a rich history of participating in prestigious competitions, both nationally and internationally, where they showcase their expertise in aeromodelling and UAV technology.

Mission and Vision

Team Phoenix aims at promoting awareness regarding the aviation sector and develop real time solutions to the ongoing issues in the field. The team shares engineering instincts to study various parameters and generate a practical layout that can be executed effectively.

Focus

Team Phoenix specializes in the design, fabrication, and innovation of advanced UAVs and drones.

Competitions

They participate in various national and international competitions, including the SAE Aero Design series.

Projects

Beyond competitions, they are involved in developing mining drones for applications like surveying, safety monitoring, and data collection in the mining industry.

Real-world Applications

The team is dedicated to finding practical uses for UAV technology in different sectors.

SAE Aero Design

They have participated in the SAE Aero Design West 2025 competition in Van Nuys, California, from April 4th to 6th.

Inductions

Team Phoenix also conducts induction processes for new members, with diverse domains like Manufacturing, Avionics, and Sponsorships.
